About Me

At Cornell University, I study Information Science (UX Design, Digital Culture) & Asian Studies with a focus on China. My work blends design, research, and narrative—from mobile games and UX systems to art and creative writing.

My global background shapes my approach: I design for diverse audiences, multilingual environments, and cross-cultural storytelling. I'm passionate about creating human-centered user experiences that address real pain points, visual designs that stand out through artistic innovation, and leveraging AI technology to support community projects.

I've had the opportunity to work for organizations like the Brookings Institution in Washington, D.C. as well as Central Pacific Bank and Hawaiian Electric in my home state of Hawaii.
